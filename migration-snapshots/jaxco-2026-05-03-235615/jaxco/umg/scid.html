There was quite an assortment of projects at UMG.  The SWAT team served a large and varied collection of business groups within both Universal Music and Universal Studios.  As design lead, I provided art direction for the team, coordinating my own efforts with those of artists on loan from other units as well as outside contractors.

The design lead was responsible for setting and maintaining the standards for art, graphics, user interface design and most front-end code for the department.  The site and interface design projects included such undertakings as a time keeping system for Universal Music employees and an online Company Store developed in conjunction with Southern California AQMD requirements.

This position afforded a great opportunity to work with many different people from every part of the business, due to the wide scope of clients and the even wider range of end-users. The client list (domestic business units) included Universal Music Video and Distribution, Universal Music Publishing, Universal Studios Corporate Communications, Verve Records, UMe, and Vivendi Universal Financials to note a few.

Many of these projects were in simultaneous production, with the design lead acting as liaison between project managers, developers, outside contractors and often the client in regards to all design, user interface, and usability issues.
