Jacqueline (better known as Jax) Patterson is Jaxco's principal, and has an extensive background in web design and development dating back to 1995. Expert in all Adobe, Macromedia and Microsoft graphics and web applications (on both PC and Macintosh platforms) including Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Flash, Visual Interdev, and IIS; as well as solid hand-coding skills (preferring to use code based vs. wysiwyg editors).

An accomplished front-end programmer capable of deploying sites that include custom html, JavaScript, cascading style sheets and active server pages, in addition to design and graphic responsibilities. Her code is as clean as her fresh, vibrant design style. She is highly experienced in designing front-ends and user interfaces for the entertainment, advertising and fashion industries, with domestic and international clients including several Vivendi Universal business units, Verve Records, Guess.com, Davis·Elen Advertising, and City of Hope.

With an exceptional ability to interpret and execute a client's verbal/written business requirements; JAXCO creates high quality, consistent designs for the Internet as well as intranets. A full service agency; well versed in all stages of web development including, but not limited to: initial requirements evaluation, definition of scope and project phases, development tools and technologies, art direction and design, content integration, QA testing, final launch and maintenance. Always ensuring that all web design and development maintains cross-platform compatibility, as many industries utilize a combination of both Windows and Macintosh hardware.
