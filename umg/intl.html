In addition to servicing domestic business units, the SWAT team was one of the few special teams designated to work with Vivendi Universal international business units in Europe and Latin America.  The images above show an intranet for Universal Music Latin America that functions as a portal, linking the seven individual country web sites that encompass the region.

Screen shots of the intranet redesign for UM3 (headquartered in London and Germany, and operating in all countries outside of North America).  As the creative lead, I worked with teams in London and Germany in addition to in-house developers to freshen the face of the project while code revisions were happening behind the scenes.  

The public web site for Universal Music Brazil.  The SWAT team worked in conjunction with both a development and design team in Brazil and the main office in Rio de Janeiro. As design lead, I reported to both the Brazilian and SWAT team project managers. This project was exciting in that it required working with translators and being able to create and modify code in Portuguese. http://www.universalmusic.com.br/
