This is the logo designed for the Strategic Web Application Technology (or SWAT) team. The team as a business unit wanted an immediately recognizable visual identity, and a way to brand it's products and applications.

Senior management requested that the imagery move away from the more obvious connotations of weapons and/or technology. The concept behind the design of the logo was to create a image that would be akin to an icon for a record label.

The lineart version was screenprinted on t-shirts and given to the team to foster department unity.
