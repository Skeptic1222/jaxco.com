Police HQ Meeting May 5

On Thursday, May 5, the city is holding a community meeting to show its plans for Police HQ Part 2 -- the Main Street Parking and Motor Transport Division Facility. (Part 1 of the plan is the Police Headquarters on the block where our Civic Park belongs!) Thursday's meeting is for the part of the plan that lands on the St Vibiana's block (right across from the Linda Lea Theatre) and which calls for tearing down the MJ Higgins gallery on Main Street. The parking structure is supposed to house 500 police vehicles; the motor pool is supposed to service (fueling, washing and maintenance)1,000 police vehicles.

If this goes through, the impact on the area -- arts, residential and business -- will be very significant. The more public comment on this the better -- please come to the meeting if you can!

Thursday, May 5 6 pm
Room 1010 (10th floor)
City Hall
200 N. Spring Street
Los Angeles, CA 90012

FYI: It is very likely that within the next few weeks -- possibly May 19 -- the entire police HQ proposal -- the headquarters building and this plan -- will again come before the city's Cultural Affairs Commission.
